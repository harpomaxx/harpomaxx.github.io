About

My name is Carlos A. Catania, but everybody calls me Harpo. (long story). I considered myself a computer geek with pretty decent social skills. I’m from Mendoza - Argentina.

I started with computers when I was eight (or nine) years old in the 80s. Back then, a friend of mine had a beautiful CZ1000 with 1K of RAM. Since then, I have always been involved in computer things. I run a BBS in the 90s (CYBERIA BBS). I helped create the Local LINUX user group at the beginning of the century (LUGMen).

Now and for the last 15 years, I have been involved in Applied Machine Learning Research.

Head of the Laboratory of Intelligent systems (LABSIN) at the School of Engineering at the University of Cuyo.

I have a Ph.D. in Computer Science (UNICEN, Argentina) and a master’s degree in Computer Networks from Mendoza University.

Interests: Machine Learning applied to navigation systems, agriculture, livestock, network security and Biology among others.
